Chest Workouts

Training your chest can help you build upper body strength and improve your posture. Here are some great chest workouts:

Exercise Sets x Reps Targeted Muscles
Bench Press 3 x 8-12 Pectoral muscles, triceps, shoulders
Incline Bench Press 3 x 8-12 Upper chest muscles
Dumbbell Flyes 3 x 10-15 Chest muscles
Push-Ups 3 x 10-15 Chest, triceps, shoulders





Leg Workouts

Training your legs can help improve your overall fitness and athletic performance. Here are some great leg workouts:

Exercise Sets x Reps Targeted Muscles
Squats 3 x 8-12 Quads, glutes, hamstrings
Deadlifts 3 x 8-12 Hamstrings, glutes, lower back muscles
Lunges 3 x 10-15 per leg Quads, glutes, hamstrings, balance and coordination
Calf Raises 3 x 10-15 Calf muscles





Arm Workouts

Training your arms can help improve your overall upper body strength and muscle definition. Here are some great arm workouts:

Exercise Sets x Reps Targeted Muscles
Bicep Curls 3 x 8-12 Bicep muscles
Tricep Extensions 3 x 8-12 Tricep muscles
Hammer Curls 3 x 8-12 Bicep and forearm muscles
Dips 3 x 10-15 Triceps and shoulders
